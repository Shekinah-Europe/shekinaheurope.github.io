Prayer Volunteers

Do you have a passion for praying for others? Do you find joy in sharing God’s love with everyone around you? You could become a valued volunteer with for Shekinah Europe.

We believe in the power of prayer, so we ask that you commit to praying for us for wisdom, discernment, and God’s perfect will in what we do. Giving at least one hour of your time weekly to pray for our intentions.

Film maker, videographer, and presenter

Do you have a passion for film making? Do you have good presentation skills? Can you commit few days a month?

We are looking for volunteer film maker, videographer, and presenter to join our team. In this role you will be working as part of small team. You need to be organised, creative and show initiative. You need to work with minimal direction and be comfortable taking the creative lead.

If Holy Spirit inspires you to take part in this journey with us, please write to admin@shekinaheurope.org
